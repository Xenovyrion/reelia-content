# About

Reelia is a personal show and movie tracking app for Android, built as an alternative to TV Time.

## What is Reelia?
Reelia lets you track what you watch: shows and movies, episode by episode, with a full history and detailed stats about your watching habits.

## Key features
- Episode-by-episode tracking, with automatic catch-up of earlier unwatched episodes
- Detailed stats: time watched, favorite genres, network breakdown, weekly and monthly charts
- Discover new titles: what's trending, suggestions based on your library, latest releases
- Sync across multiple devices via an account
- Import your existing library from TV Time
- Available in French and English

## Where does the data come from?
All show and movie information (posters, synopses, cast, ratings) comes from TMDB (The Movie Database), a free community-driven database.

## A personal project
Reelia is built by a single person, for personal use, with no ads and no data collection for commercial purposes.
